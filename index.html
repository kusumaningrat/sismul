Trawangan Island

Gili Trawangan, also known as Trawangan Island, is a small paradise located off the northwest coast of Lombok, Indonesia. It is one of the three Gili Islands, along with Gili Air and Gili Meno, and is renowned for its pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant underwater ecosystem.

The island is a popular destination for travelers seeking a laid-back tropical getaway and a haven for beach lovers, divers, and snorkelers.

Selong Belanak

Selong Belanak is a stunning beach located in the southern part of Lombok, Indonesia. It is known for its picturesque landscape, pristine white sand, and crystal-clear turquoise waters. Selong Belanak Beach is approximately 30 kilometers from Mataram, the capital city of Lombok, and is easily accessible by road.

The beach is a favorite among locals and tourists alike due to its natural beauty and excellent conditions for swimming and surfing.

Tanjung Aan

Tanjung Aan is a picturesque beach located on the southern coast of Lombok, Indonesia. It is known for its breathtaking beauty, soft white sand, and turquoise waters. Tanjung Aan is situated approximately 75 kilometers east of Mataram, the capital city of Lombok, and is easily accessible by road.

Tanjung Aan Kecil, located on the eastern side, features a crescent-shaped bay with calm and shallow waters.

Rinjani Mountain

Mount Rinjani, or Gunung Rinjani, is an active volcano and one of the most iconic natural landmarks on the island of Lombok, Indonesia. Rising to an impressive height of 3,726 meters (12,224 feet), it is the second highest volcano in Indonesia. Mount Rinjani is part of the Mount Rinjani National Park, which is recognized as a UNESCO Global Geopark.

The trek to the summit of Mount Rinjani is a popular adventure for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Nanggi Hill

Nanggi Hill is located near to Rinjani Mountain so the access is considered easy for everyone. Also, it becomes an easier sport to conduct trekking as compared to such majestic mountain.

Simply put, Nanggi becomes a perfect place for casual trekkers or those who do not want to spend much time or stamina during hiking.

Savana Propok

Savana Propok is one of the more popular destinations at Mt. Rinjani National Park. Although it is categorized as a “non-climbing” destination, visitors must still climb to reach the savanna.

Savana Propok is located at 1,700 meters above sea level. After registering at the entrance gate to the park, about 75 kilometers east of the NTB capital of Mataram, visitors must hike up to reach the savanna.

Senggigi Beach

Senggigi is a popular tourist destination located on the west coast of Lombok, Indonesia. It is known for its beautiful beaches, stunning sunsets, and vibrant atmosphere. Senggigi is situated approximately 15 kilometers north of Mataram, the capital city of Lombok.

The main beach of Senggigi stretches for several kilometers and offers a picturesque setting with soft white sand, clear turquoise waters, and swaying palm trees.
